Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Maze Mines is a fully offline mobile game. We designed it to avoid collecting personal information.

Data Collection

Maze Mines does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. The game does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, microphone, camera, or advertising identifier.

Tracking and Analytics

Maze Mines does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or behavioral profiling.

Network Access

Maze Mines is intended to run offline and does not require an internet connection for gameplay.

Local Storage

The game may store progress, settings, best results, control mode, and music volume locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Children's Privacy

Because Maze Mines does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the App Store product page.
