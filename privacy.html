Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Maze Mines is an ad-supported mobile game. Gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

Data Collection

The game itself does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, contacts, precise location, photos, microphone, or camera. Advertising SDK partners may process device identifiers, the advertising identifier when permission is granted, technical data, ad interaction data, and diagnostics to deliver and measure ads.

Tracking and Analytics

Maze Mines requests App Tracking Transparency permission on iOS 14 and later. If you allow tracking, advertising partners may use the IDFA for ad attribution, measurement, and relevant ads. If you deny tracking, ads may still be shown using contextual or non-personalized signals.

Network Access

Gameplay logic and saved progress are local. Network access may be used by advertising SDKs to load ads, prevent fraud, and measure ad performance.

Local Storage

The game may store progress, settings, best results, control mode, and music volume locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Children's Privacy

Maze Mines is not directed to children. The game itself does not ask children for personal information, and advertising SDK behavior is governed by the final ad partner configuration used in the submitted build.

Changes

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the App Store product page.
