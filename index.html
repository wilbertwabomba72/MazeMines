Maze Mines

A quiet snowy maze. Hidden mines. Pure logic.

Maze Mines is an offline logic puzzle that blends snowy maze navigation with Minesweeper-style deduction.

About the Game

Move through frozen grids, read number clues, collect lights, mark mines, and reach the green gate. Classic levels combine maze routing and deduction. Open Field mode removes the walls and focuses on mine-count calculation.

Offline LogicNo ads, no account, no internet. Just puzzle solving.
Two StylesMaze levels for routing, Open Field for cleaner mine logic.
Optional TensionShadow Hunt adds a slow pursuer that follows your trail.
